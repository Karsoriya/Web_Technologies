Tourist Destinations in Kashmir

Pahalgam

Pahalgham is situated in the beautiful Kashmir valley.It is 100km away from srinagar city.Surrounded by lush green meadows and the Lidder River. It is a perfect spot for nature lovers, trekking, and trout fishing.This place is famous for meadows,glaciers and rivers. Amarnath yatra starts from Pahalgam.

Gulmarg

Gulmarg is famous for its snow-covered slopes and is one of the best skiing destinations in India. In summer, it transforms into a vibrant meadow filled with colorful flowers, ideal for trekking and golfing.


It is 60km away from Srinagar City.It is famous for Gondola Rides, horses.

Trip to Gulmarg

Sonmarg

Sonmarg is another famous Tourist Destionation in Kashmir. It is the gateway to Ladakh.Famous Thajwas glacier of Sonmarg is the main tourist attraction. Sonamarg offers breathtaking views of the Himalayan peaks.
